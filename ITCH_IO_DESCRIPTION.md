# Kitten Python: Your Desktop's New Best Friend 🐾

**Meet Kitten Python—the tiny, pixel-perfect companion that lives right on your desktop!**

Tired of working alone? Let this little feline keep you company. Unlike other desktop pets, **Kitten Python** is smart enough to know where your windows are. Watch as it climbs on top of your browser, naps on your code editor, or chases your cursor across the screen.

---

### ✨ Key Features
*   🏠 **Window-Top Walking**: It doesn't just sit on the taskbar. It detects your open windows and uses them as platforms to roam your entire screen!
*   🎈 **Physics-Based Fun**: Watch the kitten tumble and fall with realistic gravity if you drop it or move its "platform" away.
*   🐈 **High-Definition Pixel Art**: Beautifully scaled 32x32 pixel animations that look crisp and charming on any monitor.
*   🎧 **Satisfying Purrs**: Need a break? pet the kitten with your scroll wheel to hear a soothing, high-quality purr.
*   🍕 **Interactive Care**: Keep your kitten happy! Right-click to feed it or watch it drift off into a deep sleep after a long day of "helping" you work.

---

### 🕹️ How to Play
*   **Pick Up**: Left-click and hold to carry the kitten around.
*   **Pet**: Scroll your mouse wheel over the kitten to make it purr.
*   **Interact**: Right-click for the context menu to feed your pet or quit the app.
*   **Always on Top**: The kitten stays above your windows so you never lose sight of your friend.

---

### 🛠️ Technical Details
*   **Standalone EXE**: No installation needed—just download and run.
*   **Lightweight**: Minimal CPU/RAM usage so it won't slow down your gaming or work.
*   **Privacy First**: No internet connection required, no tracking—just a cat.

---

### 💻 Open Source
Want to peek under the hood? **Kitten Python** is open source! If you don't want to buy the pre-built version, you can download the source code and build the EXE yourself for free on **[GitHub](https://github.com/cyberhirsch/Kitten)**.
