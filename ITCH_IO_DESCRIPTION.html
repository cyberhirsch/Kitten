KITTEN PYTHON

[ SYSTEM VERSION 1.0 ]

Meet your new favorite desktop companion. Kitten Python is a lightweight, non-intrusive friend that roams your screen, naps on your windows, and keeps you company while you work.

CORE MODULES

  • SMART NAVIGATION: The kitten detects the top edges of your windows, turning your workspace into a playground.
  • PHYSICS ENGINE: Features realistic gravity and falling loops if you move its platforms away.
  • PIXEL ART: Crisp, hand-crafted 32x32 animations scaled for modern displays.
  • AUDIO FEEDBACK: Interact to trigger high-quality purr sounds via the audio subsystem.

INTERACTION GUIDE

Mouse Left GRAB / CARRY
Scroll Wheel PET / PURR
Mouse Right CONTEXT MENU
Always On TOP LAYER
